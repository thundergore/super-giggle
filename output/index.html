Professional Journey

My career demonstrates a progression from customer-facing roles to data-centric positions, building expertise in analytics, visualization, and data engineering along the way.

This foundation led naturally to technical support and analytics roles at HBO Max, where data became central to understanding platform performance and user experience.

Currently at Schibsted, I work with modern data stack tools including dbt, Flyte, and Snowflake, focusing on analytics engineering and data quality.
